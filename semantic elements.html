Why your body needs excercise

Exercise improves brain health and learning. It can help people sleep better. When you exercise, your body makes chemicals that help you feel good.Exercise lowers your chances of depression and decreases feelings of anxiety. Plus,exercise can give you a real sense of accomplishment and pride at having achieved a goal — like mastering a new dance routine or beating an old time in the 100-meter dash.

Quick health tips

  • Smart Snacking: Keep healthy alternatives like carrot sticks, fresh fruit, or unsalted nuts easily accessible instead of crisps or biscuits.
  • Get 60 Minutes a Day: Aim for an hour of physical activity daily, with at least 30 minutes happening outside of school hours Activities for kids - Healthier Families - NHS.
  • Set Screen Limits: Limit TV and device time to encourage active play, lower body fat, and prevent mindless snacking Kids and Food: 10 Tips for Parents | Nemours KidsHealth.
