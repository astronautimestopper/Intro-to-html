Which of these 2 animals interest you the most

Axolotl

Type: Amphibian

Lives: Freshwater lakes and canals of Mexico city

Diet: Axolotls are carnivorous as they eat worms, insect larvae, crustaceans, and small fish

Cool fact: If an Axolotl loses any minor part of its body, it can completely regenerate the tissue without any permanent damage.

Aardvark

Type: Mammal

Lives: Savannas, grasslands, and woodlands throughout sub-Saharan Africa

Diet: Exclusively ants and termites

Cool fact:Its nose has specialized valves that automatically close while it eats, protecting its lungs from dirt and crawling insects.
